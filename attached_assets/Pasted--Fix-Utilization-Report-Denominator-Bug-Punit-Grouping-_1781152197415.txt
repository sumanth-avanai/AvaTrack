## Bug 1: Team TARGET denominator still includes 0%-target employees (CORE BUG)

### Current behavior (still wrong)
TARGET KPI card shows **1906.6h** = 173.3h × ALL 12 employees.
Team goal shows **46%** instead of 63%.
Team Util% shows **35.7%** = 681.5h / 1906.6h.

This means employees with `utilizationTarget = 0` (Christian Voigt, Veekshith
Sumanth Gurram, Punit Kumar) are still counted in the denominator — which is
wrong and dilutes both the Util% and the team goal %.

### Correct behavior

The denominator for TARGET, Util%, Alloc% KPI cards, and team Total row must
only include employees with `utilizationTarget > 0`.

```
targetHours      = SUM(availableHours) for employees WHERE utilizationTarget > 0
teamUtil%        = SUM(loggedHours, ALL employees with any hours) / targetHours
teamAlloc%       = SUM(plannedHours, ALL employees) / targetHours
teamGoal%        = SUM(availableHours × utilizationTarget/100, WHERE target > 0)
                   / targetHours
```

Employees with `utilizationTarget = 0` contribute their logged/planned hours
to the numerator ONLY if they have any hours. They never contribute to the
denominator.

### Expected result after fix (Last Month data)

| KPI | Current (wrong) | Expected (correct) |
|-----|----------------|-------------------|
| TARGET | 1906.6h | 1386.5h |
| Team Util% | 35.7% | 49.1% |
| Team goal | 46% | 63% |

Team Util% = 681.5h (all logged) ÷ 1386.5h (target>0 employees only) = **49.1%**
