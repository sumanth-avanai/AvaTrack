but "booking" in the resource planner means a future PLAN (resource_bookings).
Same word, opposite meanings — this is the core confusion. Also "Planned" and
"Allocated" are two names for the same thing, and "Remaining" means different
things in different tabs.

CANONICAL VOCABULARY — apply everywhere in these views:
- "Budgeted"  = approved total days (unchanged)
- "Planned"   = future allocations from resource_bookings
- "Logged"    = actual work from time_entries
(Reserve "Invoiced" for later — don't add it now.)
